About Me

Behind every operation
is a story worth understanding.

I'm a Computer Science graduate from UET Peshawar with a skill set that sits between two disciplines that rarely meet: the disciplined, numbers-driven world of pharmaceutical procurement, and the visual, client-facing world of design and the web.

By day, I manage the end-to-end procure-to-pay cycle in SAP MM at Ferozsons Laboratories — 150+ purchase orders a month, vendor negotiations, cost-saving initiatives across a regulated pharmaceutical supply chain.

Outside of that, I consult international e-commerce sellers on Amazon & eBay, design ad creatives and brand visuals, and build clean, responsive websites. Structure from one world, creativity from the other.
